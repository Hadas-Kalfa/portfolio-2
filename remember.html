Case Study · Remember

A Digital Memorial Platform

Redesigning “Remember” Experience

Date
January, 2025
Role
UI/UX Product Designer
Tools
Figma, Adobe Photoshop
Overview

A digital memorial platform for creating and sharing personalized memory pages.

Remember is an innovative digital memorial platform enabling the creation and sharing of personalized memory pages. In today's digital age, it offers a modern and accessible way to connect family and friends while preserving meaningful memories in an engaging and heartfelt manner.

Problem Statement

A redesign was needed to make it intuitive, accessible, and emotionally resonant for users.

Remember's digital memorial platform faced usability challenges, including a complex interface, difficult navigation, and limited personalization. A redesign was needed to make it intuitive, accessible, and emotionally resonant for users.

Research

User Research

A survey was conducted with 45 users, revealing that users often experienced frustration with the complex interface and unintuitive navigation. Due to this, there was a significant need to redesign Remember with a simpler, more emotionally supportive experience to help families preserve and share memories during difficult times, reducing stress and improving user satisfaction.

78%
experienced confusion when trying to create a memorial page
65%
found it difficult to invite and coordinate with family members due to complicated sharing process
82%
expressed a need for a warmer, more supportive design that feels appropriate for the emotional context

Design System

Homepage

The welcome screen sets a warm, comforting tone immediately using soft gradients in orange and blue to create hope and peace, avoiding the harsh technical feel of the previous version.

  • 01

    Clear Action

    Large, clear "Get Started" button — no confusion about next steps.

  • 02

    Load Reduction

    Minimal text to avoid overwhelming users during vulnerable times.

Creating Memorial Pages

A comprehensive interface with fixed header displaying name/image, interactive family tree, multimedia sections (videos, gallery, stories), favorites for cherished memories, and hobby documentation. Side menu provides quick access to all actions, creating a respectful digital remembrance experience.

Store

A marketplace for memorial-related products and services, featuring candles, flowers, and personalized items for digital and physical tributes. The interface emphasizes emotional connection through curated offerings while maintaining a respectful, commerce-enabled experience for families seeking meaningful ways to honor their loved ones.

  • 01

    Store Features Summary

    Intuitive category navigation with compact thumbnails and prominent pricing hierarchy for seamless product browsing experience.

Account

The Account section provides users with complete control over their profile and settings. The intuitive interface manages personal details, notifications, and backup preferences while maintaining visual consistency and clear hierarchy for seamless navigation.

Effortless Page Filtering

A filtering option makes finding specific pages quick and simple.

Centralized Page Editing

All editing options are conveniently located in one place on the page.

Analytics Overview

Review and analysis of views, flowers, and candles with the option of graph display.

View Stories Needing Approval

All stories requiring approval are displayed in one place, with expandable drawers for easy reading.

Instant Approval or Rejection

Approving or rejecting stories written about the deceased, with the option to contact the sender.

Public Memorial Page

The page every visitor sees balances a clear information hierarchy with warmth — presenting Remember's strengths first, then building trust through the founder's own story.
